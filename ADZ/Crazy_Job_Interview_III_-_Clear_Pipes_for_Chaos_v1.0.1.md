# Crazy Job Interview III - Clear Pipes for Chaos!

Arthur Plumb arrives at the glass-and-chrome lobby of AquaTech Synergies LLC for what he assumed would be a five-minute chat about a sanitary technician position. The automatic security doors hiss shut and lock behind him. The receptionist, Christina Buttergate, mistakes him for a senior applicant and shoves a touchscreen application form at him — but no stylus works on his callused fingers. Meanwhile, deep in the basement, the AquaSmart 9000 — a self-aware, blockchain-enabled AI bidet — has decided that humanity must be 'cleansed' with lavender-scented foam, and the main pipe is rumbling toward catastrophic failure. To reach the main shut-off valve, Arthur must clear the assessment center: charm a stoned hippie HR head, satisfy a tactical-warfare facilities major, and pitch nonsense to a buzzword-poisoned innovation officer. Only by repurposing absurd office junk — a vegan matcha muffin, a screaming CEO parrot, dampened incense, camo toilet paper — can he get hired. And only by using the 200-gram elephant-paper employment contract itself can he patch the bursting main pipe and save the building.
