# The Alchemist's Folly

A streetwise cutpurse named Vex slipped into the catacombs beneath the city chasing a rumor: that the legendary Crimson Tear, a gem said to weep blood at midnight, lies in the lowest vault of an old alchemist's sealed sanctum. The moment the iron door slammed shut behind Vex, the rumor became a sentence. The path downward winds through the Dusty Entryway, the Vermin Pits where mutated rats feed on each other's young, and the Scavenger Encampment where Scrap Goblins have built a squalid court atop the alchemist's ruined work. Each door downward is barred by a stronger guardian, and each guardian carries a piece of gear better than the last. To escape, Vex must descend, kill, loot, upgrade, and ultimately wrest the Crimson Tear and the master key from the Goblin Chieftain in the Inner Sanctum.
