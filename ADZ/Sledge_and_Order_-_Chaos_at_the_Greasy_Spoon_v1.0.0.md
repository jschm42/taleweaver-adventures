# Sledge and Order - Chaos at the Greasy Spoon

Detective Jack "Magnum" Steele arrives at the Greasy Spoon diner to locate missing informant Toothpick Charlie, last seen alive, nervous, and ordering fries. Inside, the investigation collides with a swarm of chaotic regulars and staff whose routines create constant slapstick interference. Jack must piece together Charlie's movements from contradictory testimony, ketchup clues, and absurd diner disasters. To reach the walk-in freezer, he needs to manipulate staff, obtain access to the kitchen, survive Betty's roller-skate service route, and uncover a ridiculous crime scene involving mustard storage. The case ends only when Charlie is freed, a suitably dramatic one-liner is delivered, and Jack exits while the diner erupts into meaningless background destruction.
